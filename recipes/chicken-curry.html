Chicken Curry

An easy and quick recipe of Chicken Curry.

I never tought making something this good could be so easy and quick.

To create this master peace just follow these simple steps!

Ingredients

  • Raw Chicken - 400g
  • Rice - 250g
  • Curry Paste - 3 tbls
  • Coconut Cream - 250ml
  • Salt - 1 tbls
  • Chili powder - 1 tee spoon

Steps

  1. Fry the Chicken on a pan (Don't forget to add olive oil).
  2. Cook the rice by adding double the amount of water then the rice
    Cook untill the water has dissappeared
  3. When the chicken has cooked for 7 minutes you can add the salt and curry paste, stirr and add the coconut cream
  4. Serve with the Rice
